Stars are most fundamentally recognized units of astronomical research, the building blocks of the galaxies, the basis of field of Astronomy. They are like balls which release an enormous amount of energy and heat. Their history, their formation, composition and evolution has been fascinating the scientists since the time unknown. Their age and distribution help us trace the development of the Universe. Stars are also responsible for the manufacture and distribution of elements such as Carbon, Nitrogen and Oxygen to the planetary systems that may coalesce about them. The life cycle of the star, birth and death are the basis of the field of Astronomy.
